IMC

Integrated Montessori Center (IMC) is a non-sectarian, co-educational institution that offers preschool, grade school and high school education with its main building located in Fort Bonifacio, Taguig City. IMC was one of the first ten schools in Taguig City granted permission to open classes for Grades 11 and 12 for SY 2016-2017 with the DepEd’s then implementation of the K-12 curriculum. The integration of the Montessori approach with other teaching approaches, with the inclusion of the Singapore curriculum and relevant learning materials in English, Math and Science enable the IMC student to excel academically. Evidently, its alumni are admitted and have graduated with honors from top universities in the country such as University of the Philippines, Ateneo de Manila University, De La Salle University and University of Santo Tomas.
